Hi! This website is created by me — a 17-year-old student in 11th grade from Minsk.

I’ve always been interested in English at school because it allowed me to learn from people all over the world, make friends across countries, and enjoy quality content from Western cultures.

When I had to study for my English exam, I was always bored by the tasks in textbooks. I wanted to use the language for real communication, for erasing borders — not for endless grammar drills and boring rules.

Then an idea for a modern solution to my specific goal — getting a 100/100 on my exam — started to form in my mind. Practicing past exam tasks worked best for me, but I could never make myself open the book and start working (there was always something more interesting to do). One of those distractions, of course, was spending time on my phone. “Why can’t we combine business with pleasure?” I thought. And well… now we can.

After countless sleepless nights and a few skipped lessons, I managed to create a web app that is both engaging and helped me move closer to my goal. And who knows what comes next…

Huge thanks to everyone who supports me and encourages me to keep going.

If you have any suggestions for improving this site, don’t hesitate to share them with me via email: ctcetrainer@gmail.com
